Top 5 Academic Pencils

Whether it be for writing notes or sketching pictures, these are the top five academic pencils!

1. Paper Mate Clearpoint

The Paper Mate Clearpoint is another popular mechanical pencil that is great for everyday school work. It has a 0.7mm lead that writes smoothly and is easy to erase. The pencil also features a clear barrel, which allows you to see how much lead you have left. The grip is comfortable and the pencil is durable, making it a great choice for students who need a reliable writing tool.

2. Ticonderoga

The Ticonderoga is a classic pencil that is well-loved by many. It has a smooth graphite core that makes writing effortless, and it holds up well over time. The wood casing is made from premium cedar, which means it is sturdy and comfortable to hold.

3. Staedtler Norica

The Staedtler Norica is a great all-purpose pencil that is perfect for everyday use. It has a break-resistant lead that writes smoothly and evenly, and it is comfortable to hold thanks to the hexagonal barrel. The lead is also easy to erase, making it a great choice for students who need to make frequent corrections.

4. Uni Kuru Toga

The Uni Kuru Toga is a mechanical pencil that is loved by many students for its unique rotating lead mechanism. This mechanism ensures that the lead is always sharp and pointy, which helps to reduce breakage and improve the writing experience. The pencil also has a comfortable grip and a replaceable eraser.

5. Palomino Blackwing

The Palomino Blackwing is a high-end pencil that is known for its smooth and consistent writing experience. It has a unique lead formulation that is designed to resist breaking, which makes it a great choice for students who tend to press down hard when they write. The pencil also features a replaceable eraser, which is a nice touch.

Honorable Mention: Fun Pencils

Pencils with patterns on them are a fun and stylish way to elevate your writing experience. Not only do they add a pop of personality to your stationery collection, but they also make writing more enjoyable. With a wide variety of patterns to choose from, including stripes, polka dots, and even animal prints, you can find a pencil that perfectly matches your style. Whether you're taking notes in class or doodling in your journal, pencils with patterns on them are a small yet satisfying way to bring a little joy into your everyday life.
